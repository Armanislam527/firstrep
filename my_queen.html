3D Bees Around a Flower

Bees flying in circular paths around a flower using parametric equations with Three.js

Bee Motion Equations

Each bee follows a parametric 3D circular path:

x = r1 * cos(θ + 90°)
y = r2 * sin(θ + 90°)
z = r3 * sin(θ * frequency)

Parameters are randomized for each bee to create natural-looking motion.

Bees: 5

Speed: Normal
